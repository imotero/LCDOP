LCDOP HISTORY

The organization of thae league was initiated by the Cooperative Development Authority through Adm. Soc Anthony M. Del Rosario as oversight chairman of the LGU-COOP-CDA partnership, in the close collaboration with the Office of the Provincial Governor of Cavite, with the Hon. Governor Ayong Maliksi togerther with Mr. Nelson Alindogan representing the cooperative sector.


Said partnership was forged duaring the holding of the 1st Tripartite Conaference for Cooperative Development held in Tagaytay International Converntion Center, Tagaytay City on November 26-28, 2003 with the Provincial Government of Cavite as host.


The idea of organizing the league was approved on the said event. A technical group was created to prepare a study on the mechanics, nature and function of the organization.


On November 24, 2005, during the holding of 2nd National Tripartite Conference at Bohol Tropics Resort, Tagbilaran City, various LGUs endorsed the creation of the league and elected the national officers. Dr. Emmanuel M. Santiguel, PCDO-Cavite was elected Chartered President.


On November 25, 2005,a the officers were inducted and stalled to the office with Cooperative Development Authority (CDA) Chairperson Lecira Juarez as inducting officer and it was witnessed by the CDA Administrators and a reoresentative from Department of Interior and Local Government (DILG) Dr. Mariano Gavito.


Likewise, on January 18, 2006 the officers took their oath of office before DILG Undersecretary Hon. Wenceslao Tan Andanar who recognized the league as the umbrella organization of all cooperative officers belonging to Local Government Units in the country.


In 2015, in compliance with the rules and regulations of national agiencies governing the registration and operation of organizations like the league, the LCDOP was re-registered with the Security and Exchange Commision under the name of LIGA ng mga COOPERATIVE DEVELOPMENT OFFICERS sa PILIPINAS still with the original acronym LCDOP INC. Its SEC CERTIFICATE of INCORPORATION was issued on January 21, 2015 at Mandaluyong City, Metro Manila, Philippines.


VISION

"A strong and vibrant league responsive to the needs of the Cooperative Movement and catalyst of social transformation."

MISSION

"To be a catalyst of social justic, equity and sustainable development, drawing the marginalized sectors into the mainstream of development process, thereby, giving credence to the declared policy of the state to advice cooperative development anchored on time-honored and universally-accepted cooperative principles and practices."
