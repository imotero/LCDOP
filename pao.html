PURPOSES AND OBJECTIVES

  1. To establish a strong national cooperative action agenda.
  2. To advocate for the mandatory(creation) appointment of Local Cooperative Development Officer.
  3. To develop the knowledge, skills, attitude of the Cooperative Development Officers and other cooperative development workers to become effective instrument in the delivery of the services to the cooperative sector.
  4. To assist/guide the cooperative sector and promote its interest and welfare.
  5. To develop camaraderie among Cooperative Development Officers/Workers and Strengthen the league as a truly unified organization.
