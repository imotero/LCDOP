Official Registration Guidelines

Parcitipants will consist of the following:

  • CDA Board of Administrators
  • LGU Officials and Representatives
  • Elected Local Government Officials
  • Members of Coop Development Councils
  • Provincial, City and Municipal Coop Officers or equivalent
  • Officers and Staff - Local Coop Office or Equivalent
  • CDA Regional Directors and Coordinators
  • Cooperative Officers/Members
  • Other Stakeholders/Other Sectors
